About Me

After years of consulting in the technology and innovation space, I currently work in product management, where I am responsible for end to end product development and growth across multiple technology stacks. I operate at the intersection of business, design, and engineering to build meaningful experiences that drive bottom line growth. My current role is leading the backend product team at the world's largest publisher.

When I started in the digital media industry, I worked on the frontend team, across some of the top brands in the world to optimize the frontend for SEO, user experience, and revenue. My data driven approach and solutions orientation drove millions of dollars in incremental revenue for the organization. I then moved to the data management and editorial experience team, where I run a team of multiple product managers responsible for building easy to use edit tools and migrating normalized data into the new content management system.

A creative thinker at heart, I am passionate about using technology and innovation to positively impact the user experience. I have successfully launched many products, evidence of which can be found in my products' WSJ features or in the significant revenue growth my products have generated. By championing human-centric approaches to identifying and solving the most challenging technology problems, I have helped many digital teams conceptualize, prototype, market test, and build cutting edge products.

I am constantly seeking new experiences in my professional and personal life. In my free time, I can be found running a new trail in the foothills of Colorado. As soon as ski season starts, I dedicate my Sundays to skiing new trails all around the Colorado mountains. My puppy is my partner in crime, and he accompanies me to work in exchange for fetch time at the park.
